Dates Omlette

Description

Not your typical omlette, and not quite a dessert either.

An easy recipe that will have you memorize it well before
the last bite is finished.


Ingredients
  • three Eggs.
  • small handfull of Dates.
  • two tablespoons of Butter.
  • a pinch of Salt.

Steps

  1. Mix Eggs in a bowl, add Salt,
  2. heat the Butter in the pan,
  3. add Dates, heat to golden, then flip
  4. and add Eggs, set to low and your done.
