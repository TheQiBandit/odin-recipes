Air Fryer Stuffed Mini Peppers

Description

These quick air fried stuffed mini pepper appetizers
are ready in less than 15 minutes, and require just
three ingredients.

Ingredients
  • 1 pound mini sweet peppers
  • 4 ounces prosciutto
  • 1(6.5) package spreadable cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Peppers, and preheat fryer.
  2. Cut each pepper in half lengthwise, and remove seeds and stem.
  3. Slice prosciutto into pieces the size of pepper halves,
    and lay one piece in each pepper half.
  4. Spread 1 tablespoon cheese into each pepper half.
    If prosciutto hangs over pepper sides,
    fold it upward to cover cheese.
  5. Air fry until cheese is very warm and soft,
    about 5 minutes. Enjoy!
